The Therapy Box

Project

In response to the growing mental health strain created by the COVID-19 pandemic, my team and I hacked the concept of a traditional box to create the Therapy Box. The box aims to improve mental health for individuals living in a lockdown environment. We incorporated aromatherapy and light therapy components to create a box that builds a more relaxed environment to elevate a user’s mood.

My Angle

In the age of the coronavirus, I've noticed a lot of loved ones struggle with mental health. Although society has started to progress to creating comfortable spaces to discuss mental health, a lot remains to be done. A priority for me in this project was ensuring that users would be able to seek relief with the therapy box independently until they're ready to seek help.
